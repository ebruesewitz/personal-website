My Year of Boldness

I suck at New Years Resolutions. They sound really great in theory, but I usually set resolutions that are way too ambitious and end up getting frustrated when I can’t keep them. Last year I decided that instead of a resolution, I would pick a theme that I wanted my year to be defined by. 2016 was the "Year of Boldness"—the year of taking risks and saying yes to the things that scared me.


New Year, New Me

I have mixed feelings about the traditions surrounding New Years. I love the idea of a fresh start, a blank slate, moving forward from past regrets and mistakes. It seems fitting that the New Year is often accompanied with freshly fallen snow covering the ground, symbolic of burying the past and looking forward to the future we get to begin writing for ourselves.
